Our Reason for Being

Africa does not need to wait
for the world to build its future.

We are a group of engineers, builders, and dreamers rooted in Accra — assembling the technologies that will move people, power communities, and feed nations across the continent.

The name tu-mix carries two truths: tumi — power, in Akan — and the precision of robotics. Together, they describe exactly what we are building: systems that are powerful by design, engineered to last, and built from where we stand.

We are not arriving late. We are arriving exactly on time.
